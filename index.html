I am a PhD student working at the intersection of neuroscience and machine learning at the Hertie Institute for AI in Brain Health, University of Tübingen. My research centers around probabilistic inference in mechanistic models of neuroscience. More specifically, I am applying probabilistic numerical methods and simulation-based inference to fit Hodgkin-Huxley models to experimental data.
